About Me

My Story

I'm Cubiccky (born Preside Sympathy Ndlovu), a Zimbabwean Afro-pop and Hip-hop artist, songwriter, and record producer. Born March 31, 2001, I was raised in Bulawayo and Tsholotsho before launching my music career in 2019.

My musical journey began with "Balling Hard" (2019, produced by Cliff Jeans), followed by breakthrough releases like "Die Young" (2021). Influenced by legends like J. Cole, Lil Durk, and Chris Brown, I've blended Afro-pop, Hip-hop, and Afrosounds into my unique signature sound.

What Drives Me

Connecting with listeners through authentic storytelling and innovative production. With multiple albums released in 2025 (Amasu, Amazolo, Big Namba, 73 Zhwane Love) and recent singles like "Ixesha Lami" and "Imithwalo," I'm committed to pushing creative boundaries and representing Zimbabwean talent globally.
